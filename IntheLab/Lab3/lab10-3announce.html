The Wabash River College Alumni Association is offering its winter cruise around the Hawaiian Islands. This year's cruise will disembark from Pearl Harbor in the evening after a tour of the USS Arizona Memorial. The cruise will sail around all the islands for seven days and six nights. Stopovers will allow sight seeing on many of the islands most beautiful places. Included in the cruise is a helicopter ride over the island of Hilo and the great Akaka Falls. Total cost of the cruise, including air fare from LA International is $3250. Make your reservations now.
